User's Guide

STEP 1



To register as a new user, select User from Sign Up.

STEP 2



Tap the blue colour button to mark your home location. Then fill all the details in the form and click Submit.

STEP 3



Click the link in your email to verify your new account.

Wait until admin verifies your account.

STEP 4



To login, select User from Sign In.

STEP 5



Login to your account with NIC and Password.

STEP 6



Click Personal Bin in the navigation bar.

STEP 7



Separate the your waste into containers according to these categories

STEP 8



In the personal bin, enter the amounts of your separated waste as sellable and dispose in the Personal Bin Entry and Submit.

1 Unit = Full of 10 litre bucket

STEP 9



1.You will get a SMS alert when a garbage truck is arriving.

2.Dispose your waste after the truck arrived.

3.You will get a SMS alert to login & confirm the disposed amounts.

STEP 10



Click here on the notification you see in the Home page after login into your account.

STEP 11



Enter the quantities of waste you disposed to the truck in the Disposed section and click Submit.

This is a one time submission only

Points will be added to your account after disposing. You can find the total point you have earned in the Home page.
You will find notifications in your account's Home page when Buyers book your waste to purchase.
Buyer's Guide

STEP 1



To register as a new buyer, select Buyer from Sign Up.

STEP 2



Fill all the details in the form correctly and click Submit.

STEP 3



Click the link in your email to verify your new account.

Wait until admin verifies your account.

STEP 4



To login, select Buyer from Sign In.

STEP 5



Login to your account with NIC and Password.

STEP 6



1.Find the details of total collection of waste in the area from the bar chart.

2.Find the details of individual waste details with the locations from the google map.

STEP 7



Click Booking in the navigatiion bar.

STEP 8



1.Select the garbage quantities as you prefer and set a date to pickup.

2.Click Add to List to book the waste.

STEP 9



Click the Truck icon in the navigatiion bar to view your cart.

STEP 10



Find your details about all the booked waste to be purchased.
